Rahul Gandhi's return to Parliament challengedin Supreme Court

Apetition was filed in the Supreme Court on Tuesday against the restoration of Congress leader Rahul Gandhi's Lok Sabha membership.
Rahul Gandhi was reinstated as a Member of Parliament (MP) for Wayanad, Kerala on August 7. He had been disqualified as a Lok Sabha MP on March 23 after a Gujarat court convicted him in a defamation case.
The petitioner, advocate Ashok Pandey, argued that once a member of Parliament or an Assembly loses his office due to legal proceedings, he should remain disqualified until acquitted of the charges by a higher court.
The petition said that in Gandhi's case, only the punishment had been stayed, not the conviction itself.
