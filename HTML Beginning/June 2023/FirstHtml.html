international Cricket

Kohli had endured a challenging phase in international cricket over the past three years, facing a drought of centuries during that
period. However, he ended his drought against Afghanistan in September 2022, securing his 71st international century. Since then, Kohli has been consistently scoring centuries and regaining his previous form. As a member of the WTC 2023 squad, he didn't perform well in the tournament, missing a half-century in the second innings.
