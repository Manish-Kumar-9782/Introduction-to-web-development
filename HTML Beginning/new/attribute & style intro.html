Millions of people in the United States are hit by a "historic winter storm" that has left them trapped inside their homes during the holiday season. Over 1.4 million homes and businesses are severely hit by the bomb cyclone as it has caused blackouts, power outages and dropped temperatures. Most of the outages are in the Eastern US where gusty winds have knocked down trees and power lines, reported the Associated Press.
