CirKus Movie

Ranveer Singh’s Cirkus opened in theatres today, December 23, much to the delight of his fans. The film, an adaptation of William Shakespeare’s classic play Comedy of Errors, is billed as a period comedy-drama and features the livewire in a double role. It is directed by Rohit Shetty and is his third collaboration with Ranveer Singh after Sooryavanshi and Simmba.
